# Calibration

In the Program interface, select the Calibration function with the asterisk, then press button C to enter Calibration function.

<img src="../../../../resources/C-FunctionsAndApplications/5-BasicApplication/5.2-minirobot/program_Calibration.png" width="300" align="center"/>


Select Manual to enter, use A and B keys to select the joint you want to calibrate or hold the end button to drag the joint.

<img src="../../../../resources/C-FunctionsAndApplications/5-BasicApplication/5.2-minirobot/Manual.png" width="300" align="center"/>

After selecting, the color of this area will be reversed and highlighted. Then you can use A and B keys to rotate the selected joint or hold the end button to drag the joint. Short press has a step angle of 0.1°.
After moving to the zero position you want to calibrate, press button C to save. At this time, the corresponding joint will perform automatic calibration movement, and the angle data will directly become 0° after calibration is complete.

<img src="../../../../resources/C-FunctionsAndApplications/5-BasicApplication/5.2-minirobot/Manual_J1.png" width="300" align="center"/>
