# Q&A

This chapter lists common questions when using MiniRobot to control the robotic arm for your reference.

**Q1: Can the end button on the robotic arm only be used in MiniRobot's FreeMove page or Calibration?**

A: Yes, the end button on the robotic arm can only be used in MiniRobot's FreeMove page or Calibration, and can also be controlled via the Python interface.
